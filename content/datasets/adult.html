Adult Dataset

The Adult / Census dataset

Predict whether income exceeds $50K/yr based on census data. Also known as “Census Income” dataset.

Home Page: https://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Adult

Files

  1. jspec.json: transform encode suggestion
  2. train_data.csv: training data
  3. train_data.csv.mtd: training meta data
  4. test_data.csv: test data
  5. test_data.csv.mtd: test meta data

compressed files

  1. data.zip: All the above compressed
